❓ How do I book a token?
Click on "Book Token" in the navigation, fill in your details, select a hospital, and confirm. You'll receive a token number instantly.
❓ Can I cancel or reschedule?
Yes, contact our helpline or use your confirmation email to reschedule up to 2 hours before your appointment.
❓ Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted and we use OTP verification to keep your account safe. We never share your data.
